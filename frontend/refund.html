Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on August 7th, 2025

The Habit Loop is committed to customer satisfaction. However, due to the nature of our digital services, our policies on cancellations and refunds are as follows.

Refund Policy

All purchases for our premium digital courses ("Advanced" and "Pro" levels) are final and non-refundable.

  • Once a user completes the payment and gains access to the premium course content, we cannot issue a refund.
  • Our service is a digital product that is delivered instantly upon purchase, making it ineligible for refunds.

Cancellation Policy
