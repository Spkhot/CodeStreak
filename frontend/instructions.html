Dashboard Guide

Welcome! Here’s a quick guide to help you get the most out of your dashboard and all its features.

Tracking Your Progress

Understanding Your Streak

The **Streak Status** card shows your active, unbroken streak of daily completed tasks. If you pause your course, this will reset to 0, but your course progress will be saved.

Viewing Completed Days

The **Progress Log** shows every topic you have completed. You can click on any day to review the explanation, links, and any notes you've taken (for Pro users).

Exploring the Curriculum

The **Course Curriculum** section at the bottom shows the entire 40-day plan for your selected language. Click any day to see the topic for that day.

Managing Your Schedule & Account

How to Edit Time or Number

Click your **profile avatar** (the circle with your initial) in the top-right corner of the navbar. A dropdown menu will appear with options to Edit Time and Edit Number. This will not reset your streak or progress.

Pausing Your Course

In the **Your Coding Setup** card, click the Pause Course button to stop receiving daily messages. When you're ready to start again, the button will change to Resume Course. Note: resuming will reset your **streak** to 0, but your day progress will continue from where you left off.

Editing Your Full Schedule

If you want to change your language or level, click the Edit Schedule (Reset) button. **Warning:** This action is for starting a new learning path and will reset all your progress, including your completed days and streak, back to Day 1.
